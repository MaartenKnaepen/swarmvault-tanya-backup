# Seerr — Media Requests Available

Seerr is now available via Hermes for requesting movies and TV shows!

## How it works
- Ask Hermes to download a movie or show (e.g., "download Dune")
- Hermes searches Seerr and shows you options with a picker
- You pick what you want, and it gets requested to the *arr stack
- Once downloaded, it becomes available in your media library

## Usage examples
- "download Dune"
- "download The Bear"
- "can you get me the movie Interstellar?"

## What's available
- Movies and TV shows via TMDB search
- Season selection for TV shows
- 4K request support
